Host Script: Read Before The Game Starts

All right, On Par, let me hear you make some noise!

Welcome to Pop Culture Moments Bingo. Tonight is all about big memories, iconic moments, fast fingers, and very loud BINGO energy.

To play, scan the QR code on the screen, enter your name, and choose up to three bingo cards. When a pop culture moment appears on the display, tap that square on your card if you have it. The free space is already yours.

Here is the important part: BINGOS do not end the whole game. BINGOS add points. The players with the most points at the end are our winners, so keep playing after every BINGO.

In Rounds 1, 2, and 3, any row, column, or diagonal counts as a regular BINGO for 100 points. Round 2 also has a Four Corners bonus worth 50 points. Round 3 also has an X Bingo bonus worth 200 points.

For the final round, you keep your Round 3 cards. The final round is cover-all blackout only. Fill the whole card for 500 points. Once you hit blackout, that card will be replaced with a fresh card so you can keep chasing points.

When you get a BINGO, tap the BINGO button, yell BINGO, use the noise makers on your table, and make sure we hear you. We are giving a bonus prize to the loudest table at the end of the bingo game.

So be loud, help your table, keep your cards moving, and get ready. Pop Culture Moments Bingo starts now!

Game Rules At A Glance

  • Players can use 1 to 3 cards.
  • Players may add cards on their player screen until they have 3 total.
  • Players tap squares as moments are pulled.
  • Points determine the final winners. BINGO claims add points and gameplay continues.
  • Players should yell BINGO and use the table noise makers every time they receive a BINGO.
  • The loudest table wins a bonus prize at the end of the game.
  • No new moment is pulled when fewer than 20 seconds remain in a round.

Host Dashboard Functions

Open Display Opens the big-screen display. Use this before players arrive so the QR code and countdown are visible.
Open Player Page Opens the player card page for testing or helping a guest join.
Print Host Guide Opens this printable operations module and read-aloud script.
Start 15-Min Countdown Starts the first countdown screen. Keep this up while players scan and get cards.
Skip Countdown Starts Round 1 immediately if the room is ready early.
Start Round Now Starts the current round immediately from setup or recovery.
Pull Next Moment Manually pulls a moment during a live round. Auto-pull also runs every 20 seconds.
Start 10-Min Break Ends the current round and shows the leaderboard break screen.
Next Round Starts the next round from the break screen. The final round keeps Round 3 cards.
Reset Event Clears the event back to setup. Only use this before a full restart.
Player QR Shows the player join QR and link. Players scan, enter their name, and choose cards.
Bingo Claims Shows submitted BINGO claims and point totals as players tap BINGO.

Run Of Show

  1. Open the host portal on the host device.
  2. Open the display screen on the venue display.
  3. Confirm the display QR says Scan to play and players can reach the player page.
  4. Put noise makers on all tables before the game starts.
  5. Start the 15-minute countdown and read the host script.
  6. During each round, let auto-pull run. Use Pull Next Moment only if you need to recover or speed up.
  7. When a player yells BINGO, encourage the table to get loud and remind them to tap BINGO on their device.
  8. During breaks, check the leaderboard and claims before starting the next round.
  9. Before the final round, announce: players keep Round 3 cards, final is blackout only, blackout is 500 points.
  10. At the end, announce the top winners and the loudest-table bonus prize.

Quick Troubleshooting

  • If players see a login screen, make sure they are using the public player link, not a protected preview link.
  • If the display is not changing, refresh the display screen and confirm the host portal is live.
  • If a player needs more cards, have them use Add Card on their player screen, up to 3 total.
  • If a player refreshes, their card progress should restore on the same device.
  • If a round is ending, the game will not pull a new moment in the final 20 seconds.
  • If the host needs to restart the whole event, use Reset Event and type RESET only when you are sure.
