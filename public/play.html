How points work

BINGO claims add points to your score. Points decide the final winners, so keep playing after every BINGO.

  • Rounds 1-3: any row, column, or diagonal is 100 points.
  • Round 3 allows regular BINGO plus a 200-point X Bingo bonus.
  • Keep your Round 3 cards for the final round. Final round is cover-all blackout only for 500 points. After a blackout, that card is replaced.
  • Four Corners is a 50-point bonus. The loudest table wins a prize at the end, so yell BINGO and make noise every time.
